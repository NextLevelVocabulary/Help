Filters

Filters allow you to search and pinpoint the lessons which you are looking for. You can search for lessons containing specific keywords or you can limit the search results by number of words, difficulty or age of the lesson.

Select the right combination of criteria to find your desired lesson.

When you are done with entering your selection criteria, choose the Apply Filter. The tab will change the icon color and you will be able to clearly see that a filter is applied to your search results. The filter will be saved and used consistently until you reset it.

Use the Reset Filter button to delete all filters and show unrestricted results.
