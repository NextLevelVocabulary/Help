Your First Launch

You are going to use this application for the first time and you need to establish a small daily routine to learn effectively. Here are couple important things to remember.

 

The daily learning routine is based on three different practice sessions

  1. Review practice will select few words from your dictionary every day. You will rate your knowledge and this is the only practice which changes your flashcard proficiency.

  2. Reminder practice will repeat the words which you did not know during the Review session. You will learn all the flashcards which you did not remember. The default practice quiz is Hangman and Multiple Choice.

  3. Learning practice will include few completely new words. The program will enable few new words and they will be added to your active dictionary. You will go through the same practice exercises to learn new words.

 

The Learning Process

It takes some time before you fully master a flashcard and this program uses Spaced Repetition method to help you learn faster and fight against the Forgetting Curve. You will Review each word in planned intervals, new or low-proficiency cards will be reviewed more frequently, high-proficiency cards will be reviewed less often.

By default you will start with English-Czech flashcard, than move to Czech-English level and the final level is Audio-Czech. Your proficiency changes after each Review practice and the proficiency is displayed as a little colored dot on the flashcard. You will activate the next level flashcard after the flashcard proficiency dot becomes green. More details are described in the proficiency section.

The Installation Wizard activated few thousand words for your level of proficiency and it activated one of the three flashcard levels. You need to review more flashcards in the first two weeks in order to skim all the words which you already know. You might find the first couple Reviews easy, but the more you practice, the more difficult it will get. It takes just one review to master most of the pre-set flashcards, which means the card deck will become smaller and you will start learning more difficult words soon. You will notice the difference after two weeks, the sessions will become more customized to your needs. Don't give up, learning a language is a long process.

You should spend 15-30 minutes every day to go through the three mandatory sessions. Once you complete all three practices, the next Review will not be available until couple hours. You can try the Recap activities in the meanwhile.

Few tips

You should increase the number of words in the Review during the first two weeks. Change the preferences temporarily to 150 flashcards "Review per day", which will help you to reduce the volume of words in the initial backlog. When the cards become more difficult, adjust the "Review per day" values in the Preferences.

Check your statistics page to examine your learning progress. The program will analyze your vocabulary and give you some learning tips.

If you used the two highest levels to install your dictionary (Advanced and Serious!), you will have a lot of words to review initially and don't hesitate to use the “I know it” button in the options menu.

You can enable words of interest in the dictionary. You will learn newly enabled word in the next Review practice.

Check more tips and recommendations on the best practice page.
