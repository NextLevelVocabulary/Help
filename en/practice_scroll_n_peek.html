Scroll and Peek

Display flascards in a list and check your knowledge by quickly glancing on the translation

This practice has multitude of choices to learn new words and it is up to you which one you will like the most. All flashcards are be displayed in a list and the translation is hidden on the right side.

You can learn the vocabulary using there methods

  • Peek - when you hold down the card, part of translation will be revealed and you can swipe the card to left to display more content as needed.
  • View - when you tap the very right side of the card, the whole translation will slide in and you can learn the word displaying both sides at the same time
  • Show all - show all translations from the context menu

You can scroll the list back and forth. Swiping or flinging the card to the right will remove the card from the list. You can finish the practice by removing all cards from the list or by clicking on the last entry in the list.

Pronunciation is played after a long click on the English card or by clicking on the audio icon.

 

Each line will respond differently if you tap on the left side or on the right side. Flight gesture to the right will remove the card from the list, fling left will display the translation card. Swipe gesture has similar functionality. Longpress on the card will play pronunciation for English cards.
