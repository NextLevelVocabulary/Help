Best Practices

This application is quick to use and you can review few flashcards whenever you have time. You will learn the words starting with the English word showing and you will remember the word on the other side. If you guessed correctly, you move to the next card and the program will update your proficiency for the word. You will review the flashcard couple times before the next level is activated. The Czech card or the Audio card will be activated when the proficiency level is green. It takes at least 4-5 reviews to change your proficiency from red to green.

The best part of this method is that you don't need to allow any special time to learn your flash cards. Just have your phone on you at all times during the day and whenever a small moment appears, take your phone out and drill a few cards. Such hidden moments include:

  • Waiting in line at some desk, museum or cinema

  • Commuting in public transports

  • Waiting on the phone

  • Waiting at the restaurant

The flashcards will be selected for you in order to learn the words faster. With learning, first and foremost, don’t get angry if you don’t remember something. Forgetting is normal and this program will help you to learn the words which you forget the most. Learning is a fight against forgetting. If you don’t remember the answer, don't worry, because you will see the flashcard soon again. You will probably remember the flashcard better next time.

It is important that you have fun learning new words. You will learn a lot more if you enjoy the learning. This is why the program mixes different types of cards into your daily set. Check your statistics page for some hints about your vocabulary.
