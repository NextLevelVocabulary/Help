Create new

You can create a new lesson as easy as in three steps. You will find the same instructions on the screens.

  1. Enter the lesson name and description on this page. Click the NEW LESSON button.
  2. Enter the words. You can also copy&paste any text for import.
  3. Select the words for your newly created lesson on the last page.

 

Step 1: Enter the name and description for the newly created lesson.
Step 2 : You can type or copy&paste any English text here and the program will validate the translations for you. The program will also filter out words which you already know. You will check and review the newly added words in Step 3.
Step 3 : You can now finalize the word selection. All words with colored background will be added to your newly created lesson. You can manually edit the selection by clicking on individual words. Or you can let the program to pre-select words depending on the proficiency ratings in your dictionary. Use one of the five automated filters to select the words for you.

These steps are not executed on your phone but on the Internet. Your vocabulary is automatically synchronized with your online record and the data is used in Step 3 for the automated filters.
