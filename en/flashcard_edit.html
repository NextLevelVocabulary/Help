Edit Flashcard

You can edit and update the dictionary here. Each flashcard is allowed only 3 active translations. The dictionary also contains other translation, which might be more preferrable by you. You can just select any translation from the predefined set or you can add your own translations. You can use comma to enter more translation on one line.

New translation will be inserted after you press Enter.

You can update the Part of Speech if required.

All predefined translations are sorted by the word frequency in the common language. You can change the order using the arrows on the right.

You will contribute to accuracy of the dicionary by editing flashcards. All your changes are reported and they are reviewed to make the dictionary better for other users. Thank you for your contribution.

You can save the changes by swiping the card left or right.
Changes are not saved when you press the Back button.
You can use the context menu to save/discard your changes.
