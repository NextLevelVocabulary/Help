Flashcard

A flash card is a little piece of paper, which has on one side a new word in English, and on the other side a word in Czech.

Each flashcard displays several attributes

  • English word on one side (ie. describe)
  • Multiple Czech translations on the other side (ie. popsat, nakreslit, popisovat)
  • Pronunciation icon
  • Part of speech (ie. verb)
    • Proficiency (ie. the orange dot means low proficiency)

You can usually flip the flashcard by touching it, but sometimes the flashcard can't be flipped until the practice is completed (ie. mainly the typing exercises).

Touch the Pronunciation icon to play the audio recorded by native speaker.

Fling the card left or right to move to the next flashcard or to the previous flashcard.

Every time a flashcard is displayed, you can activate three actions in the Options menu.

  1. I know It -

  2. “Exclude word from lesson”

  3. “Put the word back in the deck” and learn it later.
