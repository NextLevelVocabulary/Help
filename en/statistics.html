Statistics

The statistics page contains summary of your learning progress and few tips for your learning strategy. You can see these elements on the page

  • Summary count by proficiency levels. The words are groupped into four categories (new words, less proficient words, more proficient words and finalized flashcards)
  • Active words - this means how many words are available for your review exercise.
  • Enabled words - this means how many words are available for your learning exercise. You have not reviewed these words yet, but you will learn them at some later point in time.
