Recap Activity

Recap allows you to review the words from today session. Recap helps you learn the words even better during the time among the sessions. You forget words over time and with Recap, you can review them more frequently during the day.

 

Recap lets you choose which groups of words and which exercises you would like to use, so you can be very specific on what and how you would like to learn. You can only recap the flashcards which were reviewed or learned today. The first screen in Recap allows you to choose the groups of words for the recap session. In the next step, you can select from the checkboxes which exercises you would like to practice for these flashcards.

The best way to use this activity is to recap the words that you missed during the Review and the words that you just learned. You should allow at least two hours gap between the Recap sessions and run one or two recaps during the day in between the sessions.

For example, you can run the Review in the morning and then you can run another Recap in the afternoon and perhaps one more Recap in the evening. This way you can get the most out of the words from the daily session. You can also combine the exercises – in the morning you can do Browse Cards and Multiple Choice. In the evening you can run Browse Cards and some Typing exercise like the Hangman. It is all about consistency and by taking little steps and coming back daily, you can build long term knowledge about the words.

Keep the recap sessions very short and very specific, you can target learning only the words which you need to learn. See some best practices and other learning tips.
