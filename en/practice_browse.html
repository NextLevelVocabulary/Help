Browse Cards

Browse your flashcards back and forth until you know them

This practice is the most straightforward type of flashcard review. You can browse the cards back and forth and skip the cards which you feel comfortable. This practice is an endless loop and you will keep browsing until you skip all the cards. This practice is best used when you start with every card in the deck, narrowing down the scope to the words to the ones which are more difficult.

This practice is great in combination with your daily recap. You can mix words with different difficulty and focus your learning very well.

This practice will display the flashcards in an endless loop. If you tend to remember the words in the sequence, you can change the browsing direction to make the practice more difficult. The cards will be displayed in an infinite loop unless you use the Skip button. The Skip button will remove the flashcard from the deck and you can focus only on the words which you don't know. Browse the card deck as many times as you want, back and forth, until you remember all the words.

 

The flashcards will play the pronunciation automatically only if the English card is displayed first. The program will remember which side of the card is displayed, so use it for your benefit. I personally like to learn the English card first and the Czech card second, but I use this exercise to learn the Czech card too.

 

You can tap the card to flip it. Swipe the card to move between the cards. The buttons have same function.
