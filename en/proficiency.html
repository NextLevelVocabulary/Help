Proficiency

Proficiency is measuring the degree of your familiarity of a flashcard. The proficiency is displayed as a little dot on the card with “traffic light” colors. Each flashcard typically starts as red dot. While you will be become moring familiar with the meaning, the proficiency icon will be turn greener. Flashcard proficiency is updated only during the Review practice, based on your answers.

Proficiency for some cards is preset during the Installation Wizard. Every new card will be started from beginning with a red dot, but you will see different colors.

This is a brand new flashcard, which you manually enabled in the dictionary.
Low proficiency - this is typically a new card, you are starting to learn it. You will usually not know this flashcard very well yet. It will take typically at least 5 reviews to progress to green and complete the flashcard.
Medium proficiency - some cards are pre-set at this proficiency during installation. You might be familiar with this flashcard, but it will take at least 3 more reviews to complete it.
High proficiency - some cards are preset at this level during installation and it takes only 1 review to complete the card. You should know this flashcard very well at this point and you should be ready to enable the next level of card.
Almost-completed or Completed flashcard and the next level of card was enabled.

The flashcard proficiency and your previous answers are influencing which words you review every day. You should understand how they impact your learning progress. In general, you will review words which you know less often and you will review trouble words more frequently. This is why it's so important you come back and learn every day, because otherwise your backlog will be too big.

When you reach green proficiency, the flashcard is deactivated and you will move to the next level flashcard. For example, after you learn the Czech-English card, the Audio only card is activated.

If you know the card well and you don't want it to be shown ever again, use the "I know this word" action in the Options menu. Please use this feature carefully and only for the words which you know well as a native speaker would.
