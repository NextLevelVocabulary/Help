Practice - Your Learning Experience

All practice exercises don't have any effect on your proficiency and they help you to learn the word better. You can always use the extra exercises few times each day and try to recap words of the day. You can choose from variety of exercises

  • Three different Spelling games ( Hangman, Spell Out, Type)
  • Browse only
  • Intensive Practice
  • Scroll and Peek
  • Multiple Choice
  • Mix and Match

The practices sessions are launched automatically for Learn Missed and Learn New. You will be prompted to select the exercises for your Recap practice. You can customize your learning needs every time you run it. Every day is different and you learn faster some days and other days you struggle.
