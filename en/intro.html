Next Level English Vocabulary

This program is intended for all students who want to extend their English vocabulary. Beginners and especially advanced students will find more than 12,000 flashcards with audio pronunciation in the dictionary. You will gradually remember these words using a system of Spaced Repetition learning. This program is suitable for both beginners and advanced students, but it is primarily intended for speakers who already know English well and who want to improve their English to the next level.
 
The main features of the application
  • database of over 12,000 flashcards
    • three learning levels for each flashcard
  • pronunciation for all words (American accent)
  • adjustable speed of learning, learn at your own pace
  • many types of practice exercises and quizzes (Hangman, Multiple Choice, Spelling)
  • three phases of everyday learning (Review, Learn Missed, Learn New)
  • build your own lessons (prepare for a test next week, a new lesson in textbook, or whatever you want to learn as quickly as possible)

Read Launching the application for the first time for some practical tips for your studies.

 

This is not a crash course, which you can use to learn English miraculously in three weeks. If you have been learning English for couple years, and if you want to take your English to the next level, you can use this program to learn more effectively. You will cut down the time required to remember large volumes of vocabulary and remember it permanently.

 

The program records your answers, and it is based on Spaced Repetition learning method to fight against the Forgetting Curve. New or forgotten flashcards will be repeated more frequently, and well-remembered flashcards will be reviewed at longer intervals. Timely review will effectively increase your proficiency and build long-term knowledge fast. The program creates a new flashcard set for every day Review Practice. The review words are based on your previous answers and the word proficiency. This program works best if you use it everyday - just use it for a few minutes on the way to school or to work, and you will see the results in just a few weeks. You will learn by completing short daily lesson and by focusing on new words or words which need more attention. Read the best practices section for few tips.

*This is applicable for the next major release: This application is best used in combination with classical learning from a textbook. You can create your own lesson from the textbook, and you can learn this lesson as often as needed. This way you will be able to memorize new words for a quiz or test and create short-term knowledge. This program will take care about your permanent knowledge after the long-term learning process will kick in.
