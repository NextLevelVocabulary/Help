My Vocabulary

There are many inactive flashcards in the dictionary. You need to activate the words if you want to start learning them. This screen is used to select individual words for your leaning sessions.

You can also edit the flashcards here. If you click on the translation, you can modify the pre-selected translations or you can add your own translation.

 

Click the flag to change the translated language.

The results will display your proficiency levels as a traffic light for all three types or flashcards.

 

Below is a short explanation for each status:

You will eventually learn all words in the dictionary, but Inactive words are not yet included in your learning flashcard set. Activate these words and you will start learning the flashcard immediately.

When you click the “Active” button, the word will be automatically added to your flashcard set and you will review this flashcard during the next session.

You can use this feature to learn specific words with priority. For example, when you want to learn right away words which you just learned at school or when you need to study for a specific test. This is further enhanced in the My lessons, where you can review whole lesson easily and target your learning experience more precisely. If you need to learn for a test or an exam, My lessons is the best way to get prepared.
You can "Disable" words to exclude them from future Reviews. Disabled word will not be used for your learning exercises or your review exercises.
Words are labeled as "Completed" if you finalized all three levels of the flashcard.
