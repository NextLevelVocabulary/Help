Hangman

Guess letters.

This is a slightly modified version of classical hangman. There are only winners in this game and nobody gets hanged. The rest is classical Hangman practice. You will always guess the foreign word and the flashcard will even let you play the pronunciation. The word to guess is represented by a row of dashes and the flashcard will show the category of the word (part of speech). The correctly guessed letters are written in its correct positions.

Use the pronunciation if you are struggling to remember. You can use the hint button as the last resort â€“ it will guess a correct letter for you. Swipe the card right to move to the next card (only available after the flashcard is fully completed).
