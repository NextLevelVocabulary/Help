Mix and Match

Match words and their translations

You will be matching words and their translations in this practice. All English cards are in the left column and their translations are in the right column. The goal is to match pair of cards.

There are two levels of this practice

  • easier - which reveals the answers immediatelly and it does not allow you to match incorrect pairs
  • normal - you have to match all cards on the screen before the results are revealed.

 

You can drag the cards to match them. The cards will align and change color while dragging, once you release the card the program will attempt to match them.

 

You can also tap the cards and match pair of cards by tapping two cards in different columns. Cards will turn yellow when you tap them.

 

Matched cards are always displayed as green.

Incorrect matches are temporarily marked in red color.

Matches which are awaiting evaluation are displayed in blue.
