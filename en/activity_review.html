Review Activity

Review is the first activity of the day and the only one changing your proficiency. Answers during Review will affect your learning curve for each word.

 

The program will prepare a mix of new cards every day based on spaced repetition learning. You will assess how well you know the flashcards. Your answers will determine when you review the card again, because the program will tailor the speed to your learning performance. This program uses spaced repetition, which means that the words which you remember easily are scored more highly and are repeated less and less frequently, whereas words that are more difficult are repeated more frequently - hopefully until they are well known and remembered.

 

In order to make your daily learning session flow nicely, the daily exercises are configured in the user preferences. The application will not prompt you for the daily practice and the exercises will be executed in the order defined in the user preferences. To change the practice exercises, go to preferences for more details.

 

Your will be dealing with the forgetting curve on daily basis, so this is why it's important to use this program couple times a day.

The set of flashcards for your review session is generated daily freshly. Your review session needs to be difficult, but also easy and enjoyable at the same time. This way you will keep coming back everyday and you will keep learning.
