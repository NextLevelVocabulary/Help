Preferences

Preferences are split into four tabs.

Preferences - Setup your flashcards and Review sessions

You can adjust the volume using the slider or the volume buttons. Sound effects will enable or disable sound feedback during the exercises, for example; in Multiple choice sounds are used to give you feedback if you chose the right answer or not. If you want to disable the pronunciation, adjust the volume to 0.

  • Reviewcards per day – select how many cards you want to review in the Review activity. This depends how much time you have dedicated for learning each day.

  • Learncards per day - select how many cards will be included in the Learn New activity. This option lets you customize how quickly your dictionary fills up and how many new words you will learn each day. You might need to increase the values initially. You might not be able learn more than 10 words a day in the long term, but you can increase this value temporarily to boost your learning progress.
  • Missed cards per day – select the maximum cards you can miss during the Review activity. This is useful if the words in your dictionary are becoming very difficult to learn. You can limit how many words you can miss in one day. This is particularly important if you are learning on a bus or subway and you have limited time to learn, because the more words you miss, the longer it takes to learn them.

Preferences – Part of Speech and Skill Levels

Nouns and verbs are the most common words in the dictionary. Nouns are usually easier to learn than the other words. This filter only applies to the words in your learning session.

If you are learning new language, it is easier to learn nouns first, verbs second and and the remaining categories quickly after. This approach can give you a nice kick-start in learning a new language.

Preferences – Games and Exercises

This selection is for your default practice exercises. You can select which exercises you will be practicing by default and you can also modify their order. Drag up or down each line to modify the sequence and click the button on/off to enable/disable the practice. This filter only applies to the words for your learning session. The program will use your preferences to automatically run your learning practice sessions. These exercises are automatically started for your learn session, but you can modify your selection during the recap process.

Recap activity can be customized before each practice, depending on your needs for the day.

You will probably like the Browse Cards and Multiple Choice for new words, but use the typing exercises for learning the words which you want know little bit more.

Preferences – Various settings

You can customize the sequence in which the flashcards are activated. The default settings are imported the way I like it. My preference is to start with English to Czech card, because I want to be able to understand English words first. Once I am comfortable understanding the words, I want to be able to express myself and I want to learn the Czech to English card. At last, I want to see if I understand spoken language and that's why the Audio card is activated.

You can customize the learning sequence the way you like it. Drag the line to change the order of the flashcards and click the On/Off button to disable the level if necessary.

Changes to flashcard levels are not visible immediatelly. These changes will be applied when the next Review session is generated.

Reset preferences button will enable all categories which have previously disabled. This action will not modify any profficiency settings or previous results, this process will only enable all the words and all the categories for your learning exercise.
The program collects various anonymous statistics about the dictionary. If you allow to send anonymous statistics, the data will be used to make the dictionary better and more accurate. The program collects details such as your edit to the flashcards or the frequency which words were reviewed when in order to adjust the algorithms and learning experience.
