My Lessons

My Lessons page will display all lessons downloaded to your phone or tablet. All downloaded lessons can be learned at any time and all words will be added to your active vocabulary.

Your learning should be focused and your should avoid learning too many words at once. This is why you can only download 50 lessons as a maximum to your phone or tablet. All your lessons will remain active on the web, which allows you to return to your previous lessons if needed.

Tip : Keep your lessons limited to a manageable volume and do not “try to boil the ocean”. Trying to learn too many words at once will not result in long-term word retention. My Lessons is meant to boost your short-term memory retention and you should switch to the long-term daily learning within a month. Delete old lessons and rely on the “Spaced repetition” algorithm to learn the words at the right intervals to build long lasting knowledge. Use the Date Filter to display older lessons and delete unused records to maintain your lessons updated.

 

Swipe the lesson to the right to start learning it.
Click the lesson to display more detailed description page.
Swipe the lesson the the left to delete it from the phone.
Change the Filters to limit the number of lessons displayed.

Public on web / Private of web

This screen will allow you to browse the web-based lesson catalog. The web catalog contains all your private past lessons and also all the public lessons from other users. The private lessons are only visible to you, public lessons are visible to everybody. All lessons can be downloaded to your phone, but remember the 50 lesson limit in your device.

Lessons can be organized into folders. The lesson icon and the folder icon have different colors depending on their status.

Green Icons
Lessons and folders downloaded to your devices are displayed in green color. The green color is used for everything that is based locally.
Blue Icons
Data stored on the web is displayed as blue. All lessons uploaded by you or other users to the Internet will be blue, but the icons will turn green when you download the lesson to your Android device. The blue color is used for everything which is not stored locally.
Red Icons
If the lesson was updated since you last downloaded it, it will be displayed in red color. You will be able synchronize the changes with your phone if you want to.
